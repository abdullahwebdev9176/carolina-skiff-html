About Us

Carolina Skiff Story

As North America's leading manufacturer of fiberglass outboard boats, Carolina Skiff LLC delivers 100% composite, wood-free vessels built for performance and durability. From the versatile Ultra Elite to the shallow-draft LS and JLS Series, our NMMA-certified center consoles offer unmatched stability and value. Whether for professional angling or family fun, trust the brand that has defined quality and reliability on the water for over 40 years.
